Features

Everything a serious reader needs

Built for people who read intentionally, not algorithmically.

📡
Any RSS or Atom feed
Subscribe to any publication. Import your library in seconds via OPML from Reeder, NetNewsWire, Feedly, or Inoreader.
✨
AI summaries in one tap
Claude reads every article and gives you 3–5 sharp bullet points. Understand the story in 10 seconds before committing to read more.
🏷
Smart keyword buckets
Define keywords like "AI", "climate", or your company name — Feedbox auto-collects matching articles into virtual feeds across all sources.
✍️
Highlights and notes
Select any passage to highlight in four colors. Add Kindle-style notes. All saved to your personal database, searchable forever.
🔍
Full-text search
Search your entire reading history with Postgres full-text search. Find any article you've ever read, instantly.
📖
Auto full-text fetch
Automatically fetches the complete article for paywalled or truncated feeds. Clean typography, no ads, no noise. Just the words.
⏱
Read Later queue
Save articles with one keystroke (L). Never lose an interesting piece to an overflowing inbox again.
⌨️
Full keyboard control
Navigate with J/K, open with O, mark read with R. Power users stay in flow without touching the mouse.
☀️
Light & dark themes
A refined dark theme from the brand palette. Easy on the eyes for long sessions. Follows your system preference automatically.
