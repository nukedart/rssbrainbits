How it works

Up and running in under two minutes

No RSS expertise needed. If you have a list of sites you follow, you're already done.

01
📡
Add your sources

Paste any URL and Feedbox finds the feed automatically. Or import your entire library from Feedly, NetNewsWire, or Inoreader via OPML in one click.

02
🏷
Create smart feeds

Define keywords like "AI", "climate", or your company name. Feedbox scans every article across all your sources and auto-collects matches into a virtual feed.

03
✨
Read in seconds

Tap any article for an instant 3–5 bullet AI summary. Decide in 10 seconds whether it's worth a full read. Your attention stays where it belongs.

Features

Built for people who read with intention

Not for doomscrollers. For the people who actually finish articles.

✨
AI summaries in one tap
Claude reads the full article and writes 3–5 sharp bullet points. Understand the story in 10 seconds. Go deep only when it's worth your time.
🏷
Smart keyword feeds
Create virtual feeds from keywords across all sources. Follow "Series A", "climate legislation", or your own company name — never miss a mention again.
📖
Full article, no paywalls
Truncated or paywalled feeds get automatically fetched in full. Clean typography, no ads, no noise — just the article as it was written.
✍️
Highlights and notes
Select any passage to highlight in four colors. Add Kindle-style margin notes. Every highlight is saved to your personal database and searchable forever.
🔍
Full-text search
Find any article you've ever read with Postgres full-text search. Your reading history becomes a second brain, not a black hole.
⏱
Read Later queue
Press L to save any article for later. Never let something interesting get buried under tomorrow's inbox. Work through your queue at your own pace.
⌨️
Full keyboard control
J/K to navigate, O to open, R to mark read, L to save for later. Power readers stay in flow without lifting their hands from the keyboard.
📊
Reading stats
Track your reading streak, weekly volume, and 30-day history. Build a reading habit you can actually see — and feel proud of.
☀️
Light and dark themes
A refined dark theme built from the brand palette — easy on the eyes for long sessions. Follows your system preference automatically.
